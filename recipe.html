Chilli Garlic Noodles with Crispy Tofu

Ingredients

  • Egg Noodles
  • Firm Tofu
  • Cornstarch
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Chilli Oil
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Stir-fry Veg
  • Garlic
  • Brown Sugar

Instructions

  1. Remove water from tofu noodles, slice into 1-inch thick slabs and place on a plate, with parchment paper and a heavy book on top (in that order) for 30 mins.
  2. Boil the egg noodles.
  3. Heat some chilli oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce and brown sugar in a pan.
  4. Crumble the tofu and mix with cornstarch. Fry with sunflower oil until crispy.
  5. Take the crispy tofu out of the pan and fry the veg.
  6. Take the veg out of the pan and put the sauce from earlier back in. Add crushed garlic and ginger.
  7. Add the noodles to the sauce.
  8. Add the veg and tofu to the sauce. Mix everything thoroughly.
  9. Plate the dish up and serve!
